Kaitiakitanga & Plastic Pollution

What is Kaitiakitanga?

Kaitiakitanga is a Maori concept which refers to the guardianship and protection of the environment and its natural resources. Kaitiakitanga is linked to plastic pollution as Kaitiakitanga strives to stop plastic pollution for the betterment of the environment.
