About Kaitiakitanga & Plastic Pollution:

How does Kaitiakitanga relate to
Plastic Pollution?

Kaitiakitanga relates to Plastic Pollution because it is about protecting and caring for the environment. Plastic pollution affects many aspects of our environment negatively, affecting and harming our land, waterways, and animals. The effects of plastic pollution goes against the values of Kaitiakitanga of protecting and caring for Earth and its environment. By following the values and ideas of Kaitiakitanga, we would help in the reduction and clearing of plastic waste. It's not entirely about ridding the use of plastic entirely, but respecting the Earth and maintaining it for the future (A key value of kaitiakitanga). Fighting against plastic pollution is a key aspect of acting as guardians of our environment.
