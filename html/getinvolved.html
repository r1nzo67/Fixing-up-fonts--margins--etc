How can you get involved?

There is a number of ways you can get involved in the fight against Plastic Pollution using the values of Kaitiakitanga:

  • Participate in local clean-up events.
  • Reduce your plastic usage by opting for reusable products.
  • Educate others about the importance of protecting our environment.
  • Support policies and initiatives that aim to reduce plastic waste.
  • Join community groups focused on environmental conservation.
  • Advocate for sustainable practices in your local area.
  • Engage in discussions about environmental issues on social media.
